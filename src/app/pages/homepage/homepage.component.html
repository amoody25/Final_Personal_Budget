Manage your budget with ease!

Simply click on Dashboard and begin adding items to your budget.

The budget table will display your budget items and allow you to manage your budgets.

The expense table allows you to track and manage any of your expenses.

Simply add the merchant you spent money with and add one of your budget categories.

Finally enter the amount you spent and the table will update with your new expense.

Below the tables you will find a chart that displays your budget compared to your expenses.

This is a great way to see how much of your budget is left!
